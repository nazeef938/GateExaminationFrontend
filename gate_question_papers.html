GATE Question Paper includes 65 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), multiple select questions (MSQs) and Numerical Answer Type (NATs). As per GATE Exam Pattern, 15% weightage of marks is given to General Aptitude section, 13% to Engineering Mathematics, and the remaining 72% to the core subject paper. However, for disciplines like the AR, CY, EY, GG, MA, PH, and XL, there are only two sections in the question paper i.e. General Aptitude section carrying 15% of the total marks and the remaining for the specific discipline.
